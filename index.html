Welcome, I'm Toma.

I'm interested in creating physical and digital systems to track our information streams and ultimately create biopersonalized algorithms by integrating physiological data. You can find some of my writing related to these topics.

Currently, I'm working on Lifestack by DeMind Inc. as a co-founder and head of product.

I'm a PhD dropout, who worked on various Human-Computer Interaction and Neurotechnology projects at Columbia University. Previously, I was an undergrad at the Unversity of Washington, exploring Ubiquitous Computing, Neural Engineering, and Entrepreneurship. I am also a Neurotech enthusiast, previously led Synaptech and wrote articles with NeurotechJP.
