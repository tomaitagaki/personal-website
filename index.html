Welcome, I'm Toma.

My mission is to improve implicit interactions between humans and computers through personalized algorithms and cost functions. Previous explorations include physical and digital systems to track our information streams and and create biopersonalized algorithms by integrating physiological data.

I am currently exploring the future of human-computer interaction in a post-AGI world. Learn more about implicit here.

I helped found Lifestack by DeMind Inc. as a co-founder. I'm a PhD dropout, who worked on various Human-Computer Interaction and Neurotechnology projects at Columbia University. Previously, I was an undergrad at the Unversity of Washington, exploring Ubiquitous Computing, Neural Engineering, and Entrepreneurship.
