HydroSense Mini

Developing a high-adherence smart water bottle to study personal hydration. Three main aims: 1) Utilize active capacitive sensing to measure water levels reliably. 2) Integrate kinetic energy harvesting to eliminate the need to charge the device. 3) Track personal hydration habits alongside physiological signals to find markers of dehyrdation.
